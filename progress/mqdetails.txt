✅ Pros
Ultra-low latency (best in class)

Built-in request/reply pattern

🥇 If your priority is LOW LATENCY (on-prem):

🥈 If your priority is durability + replay + scale:

👉 Choose RabbitMQ

👉 Choose Apache Pulsar
